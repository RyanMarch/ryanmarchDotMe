Furniture Bank Promo

Non-Profit Promotional Video

Directing, Production & Editing • November 2015 • Furniture Bank of Central Ohio

The Project

This promotional video, produced for the Furniture Bank of Central Ohio, details how individuals can get involved through volunteering and tells the powerful story of how the non-profit organization serves the Greater Columbus community. It features deep interviews with President Steve Votaw, Volunteer Manager Colin Veatch, and dedicated volunteer Barbara Powell.
