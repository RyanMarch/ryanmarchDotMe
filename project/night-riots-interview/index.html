Night Riots Interview

Interview & Podcast

Apr 2015 | WXCU Radio

Out on tour with The Mowgli’s, alternative rockers Night Riots played at the A&R Music bar in Columbus on April 7th. WXCU DJs Ryan and Maura had a chance to talk with Matt (guitar, keyboard) and Mikel (bass) before the show. Questions ranged from favorite tour foods, the writing process of their newest EP Howl, and even Warped Tour Prom. Take a listen to the interview and check out the band at nightriots.com.
