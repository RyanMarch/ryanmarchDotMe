The Interview

Out on tour with The Mowgli’s, alternative rock band Night Riots played at the A&R Music Bar in Columbus, Ohio on April 7, 2015. WXCU DJs Ryan March and Maura O’Donnell had a chance to sit down and talk with Matt (guitar, keyboard) and Mikel (bass) before the show. Questions ranged from favorite tour foods, the writing process of their hit EP Howl, and even their memorable Warped Tour Prom experiences.
