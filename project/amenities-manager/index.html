The Product

The RentPress Amenities Manager Add-on provides property marketers with precise control over the amenities listed on their websites. While the default RentPress sync delivers a one-to-one representation of a property's software feed, the Amenities Manager allows for fine-tuning that sync to handle real-world data inconsistencies and marketing preferences.

Whether a property needs to keep up with a frequently-changing amenity list or completely block specific items from appearing publicly, this add-on provides the necessary tools to maintain a clean and professional listing.

Official Plugin Banner

Key Features

  • Advanced Sync Options: Select from three distinct sync methods—Default, Unassociate, or Delete—to ensure the website always accurately reflects the property's latest data.
  • Granular Visibility Control: Individually block specific amenities from displaying on the front end, ensuring that internal-only data or redundant features stay hidden from prospective renters.
  • Floor Plan & Unit Features: Beyond property-wide amenities, users can also manage visibility for specific floor plan and unit-level features.
  • Content Management: Handles landing pages for each amenity, facilitating easier SEO optimization.
  • User-Friendly Settings: A dedicated settings page within the RentPress menu that makes it easy to manage even the most extensive amenity lists.

My key contributions included:

  • Backlog Management: Defined the functional requirements and prioritized the development roadmap, ensuring a high-quality and stable product release.
  • Documentation: Authored comprehensive support documentation and "how-to" guides to empower users to safely use advanced sync options without data loss.
  • Quality Assurance: Collaborated with engineering to ensure the add-on maintained performance standards during large-scale property data imports.
