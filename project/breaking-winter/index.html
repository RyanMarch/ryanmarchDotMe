Breaking Winter

Original Composition

Audio Production & Composition • April 2014

The Project

Breaking Winter is a relaxed song with a hip-hop inspired beat. With Breaking Winter, the song feels melodic and atmospheric, but does not have an obvious, distinct melody. This piece provided the perfect opportunity to experiment with layering synthesizer arrangements and musical concepts. The song features extensive use of automation, and production techniques learned from other projects.
