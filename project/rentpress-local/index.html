The Product

Over 90% of apartment hunters start their journey on search engines or mapping apps, not on specific property websites. RentPress Local is designed to meet those prospects exactly where they are. It’s a local listings management tool that syncs real-time data from top Property Management Systems (PMS) directly into over 70 digital services, including Google Maps, Apple Maps, Facebook, and Yelp.

By providing a single source of truth, it eliminates the need for property teams to manually update office hours, photos, pricing, and availability across dozens of disparate dashboards.

Meet Searchers With The Right Info - Every Time

Key Features

  • Automated Sync: Pulls real-time property info from major property management systems like RentCafe, Entrata, Funnel, AppFolio, and MRI MarketConnect.
  • Data Consistency: Automatically updates profiles across 70+ directories, social networks, and navigation apps.
  • Local SEO Boost: Synced listings ensure data consistency, which helps Google My Business pages and property websites rank higher in local search results.
  • Review Management: Centralized tools to monitor, share, and encourage online reviews from residents and prospects.

My Role & Impact

As the Product Manager, I led the end-to-end development of RentPress Local, positioning it as an essential tool to extend a property's SEO presence and overall reach. I guided the product from conception to launch by interfacing with third-party partners, the internal SEO team, and leading the engineering team.


My key contributions and the product's impact included:

  • API Partnerships & Architecture: Managed partnerships with major property data systems and Yext. I evaluated complex API data and defined the technical requirements for our platform to digest PMS data and relay it effectively to the Yext API for broadcast to Google Maps and 70+ other services.
  • Internal Efficiency: By automating the data flow and integration process, we successfully streamlined our internal onboarding times from several weeks down to just about an hour.
  • Cross-Functional Leadership: Made core product decisions, led engineering sprints, and collaborated heavily with the internal SEO product team to ensure the product maximized local search visibility.
  • Go-To-Market & Documentation: Authored technical documentation and wrote the public-facing marketing materials, clearly communicating the value of a centralized "source of truth" to property managers.
  • Customer Empowerment: Delivered a solution that gave property managers full control over their maps presence directly from software they were already using, ensuring apartment shoppers always saw accurate, up-to-date office hours and property details.
