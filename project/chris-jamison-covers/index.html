Treasure (Bruno Mars Cover)

A high-fidelity studio cover recording of Bruno Mars' smash hit "Treasure," performed by the exceptionally talented vocalist Chris Jamison (who later became a finalist on NBC's The Voice). The track was recorded, mixed, and mastered by Ryan March in December 2013, capturing the vibrant energy, funky basslines, and crisp vocals of the original while adding custom analog-modeled warm harmonics.

Unaware (Allen Stone Cover)

A soulful studio cover of Allen Stone's "Unaware," showcasing the phenomenal dynamic vocal range of singer Chris Jamison. This acoustic-led cover was recorded, mixed, and mastered by Ryan March in December 2013, focusing on warm vocal presence, balanced room acoustics, and a pristine acoustic guitar soundstage.
