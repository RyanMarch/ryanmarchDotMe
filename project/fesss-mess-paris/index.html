Fess's Mess - "Paris"

Studio Cover Recording

Audio Production & Mixing • April 2013 • Fess's Mess

The Project

A bluesy and hard-hitting studio cover of Grace Potter & The Nocturnals' hit song "Paris (Ooh La La)," performed by Columbus local band Fess's Mess. This project, recorded, mixed, and mastered by Ryan March in April 2013, highlights crunchy distorted guitar tones, punchy acoustic drums, and a driving rock groove.

Single artwork for Fess's Mess's cover of "Paris".
