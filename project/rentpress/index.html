The Product

RentPress for Websites is a powerful, extendable WordPress plugin that transforms any website into a high-performance online leasing office. Built for the multi-family housing industry, it delivers real-time pricing and availability from leading property management software directly into native, SEO-friendly WordPress elements.

The Market

When an apartment shopper visits a community website, they immediately want to know two things: what's available and what it costs. Because rent prices change daily based on complex internal management systems, property websites often rely on clunky, un-stylable iframes or kick users out to third-party portals.

RentPress allows anyone to create a modern, flexible website for an apartment community, built for any device a shopper may use to visit.

Key Features

Over its lifetime, RentPress evolved into a robust ecosystem. Some of the standout features I helped bring to customers include:

  • Real-time Data Sync: Integrations with 12 major multifamily CRMs to keep property, floor plan, unit, and customer information up to date.
  • Advanced Search & Filtering: Dynamic and customizable pages allow marketing teams to create unique search pages for properties and floor plans.
  • Deep Integrations: Expanded capabilities with Google Maps and Mapbox for location browsing, Engrain SightMap for interactive floor plans, and Google Analytics for in-depth shopper journey tracking.
  • Lead & Data Control: Purpose-built extensions for seamless lead delivery and retrieval.
