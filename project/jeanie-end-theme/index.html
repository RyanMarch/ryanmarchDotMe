Jeanie - "End Theme"

Music Video

Directing, Production & Post-Effects • March 2015 • Big Guap Productions

The Project

A highly stylized music video for Jack Jeanie's "End Theme" featuring eye-catching experimental visuals, creative storyboarding, and custom color-graded digital video effects. The video was filmed on location with multi-camera setups to establish a unique atmospheric tone.
