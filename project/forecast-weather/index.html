Forecast Weather

Windows App

Feb 2013 | Microsoft

This app was created in a day at a workshop held at Capital University. The event was hosted by Brian Prince, Principal Cloud Technical Evangelist at Microsoft (a key player in Azure online services), and a Capital alum.

I wrote this simple Windows 8 app in the Touch Develop system. The app delivers a 7-day forecast based on a user-defined location. It was coded in a day and released to the app store. I also submitted the app to Microsoft's Monthly App Challenge as part of the DreamSpark Program and was selected as a winner for the month.

The app was removed from the Windows app store in September 2016.


Award Winner

Forecast Weather was selected as a winner of Microsoft's DreamSpark Monthly App Challenge.
