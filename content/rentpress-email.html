RentPress Email

Automated Lifecycle Email Marketing

Senior Software Product Manager

The Product

RentPress Email is an automation product that connects lead information from property management software directly to Mailchimp. It solves one of the most common problems in apartment marketing: data silos. Traditionally, leasing teams had to manually export CSV files from their management software just to send a basic email blast. We replaced that manual work with a continuous sync engine that keeps prospect info updated in real-time.

By automating the data flow, we allowed marketing teams to move away from generic blasts and toward personalized journeys that reflect where a shopper is in their search.

Key Features

  • Automated Contact Management: Real-time sync of lead data from property management systems like Entrata, RealPage, and others. No more manual exports or outdated lists.
  • Multi-Property Support: A tag-based system tracks "portfolio shoppers." When someone shows interest in multiple properties, we add a tag for each, allowing us to trigger property-specific automations from a single account.
  • Lead Status Tracking: The system continuously updates a contact’s status as they move from lead to applicant to resident. This ensures our messaging is always accurate and relevant.
  • Smart Personalization: Emails are automatically populated with data points like bedroom preferences, pet status, and move-in dates, making the content feel tailored to the individual.
  • Conversion Automations: Set up automated welcome series and follow-ups that adapt based on the information the prospect has already shared.

Improving Shopper Experience

A major pain point for properties was sending "Apply Now" emails to people who had already applied. By automating the lead status sync, we ensured that prospects were moved out of marketing tracks the moment they converted. This saved on email costs and prevented a confusing first impression for potential residents.
