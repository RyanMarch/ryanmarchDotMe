Furniture Bank Promo

Non-Profit Promo

Nov 2015 | Furniture Bank of Central Ohio

This video, produced for the Furniture Bank of Central Ohio, shows how to get involved with volunteering and tells the story of how the non-profit organization helps the Greater Columbus community. Persons interviewed include President Steve Votaw, Volunteering Manager Colin Veatch, and volunteer Barbara Powell.
