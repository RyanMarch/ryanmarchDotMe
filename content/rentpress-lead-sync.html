The Product

RentPress Lead Sync Add-on bridges the gap between a property's website and their leasing office. Experience seamless lead delivery at the moment it matters most: getting that lead into the CRM quickly and accurately is what drives leases.

This add-on allows marketers to use the power and flexibility of popular form-building tools while ensuring every submission flows seamlessly into industry-standard CRMs without requiring custom API development for every site.

Automated Lead Flow From Website to CRM

Key Features

  • Broad CRM Support: Direct integrations with the most popular multifamily CRMs, including RentCafe, Entrata, Knock, Funnel, AppFolio, and Anyone Home.
  • Flexible Field Mapping: A custom interface to map any form field to CRM-specific attributes, including custom lead sources and move-in dates.
  • Multi-Destination Routing: Send a single form submission to multiple CRMs or notification services simultaneously.
  • Advanced Troubleshooting: Built-in API response logging and submission tracking to ensure no lead is ever lost.
  • Dynamic Data Population: Automatically pass property, floor plan, and unit-specific data from RentPress templates into hidden form fields for better attribution.

My Role & Impact

As the Product Manager, I defined the roadmap for our integrations ecosystem. My focus was on reviewing the details of multifamily APIs and turning them into a user-friendly interface that anybody could easily configure.


My key contributions included:

  • API Strategy: Researched and documented the technical requirements for each CRM partner, managing the authentication and data mapping logic for multiple enterprise-scale APIs.
  • Product Development: Led the engineering team through the development of the mapping UI, ensuring it was flexible enough for power users and simple enough for standard property setups.
  • Conversion Optimization: Implemented the "API Response" logging feature, allowing clients to see why a lead might have been rejected by a CRM (e.g., missing required fields), which significantly reduced "lost lead" support tickets.
  • Documentation & Enablement: Authored the comprehensive support documentation and training materials to empower our operations team to deploy lead sync across hundreds of sites.
